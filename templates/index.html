System Features

Everything you need to efficiently manage a modern hostel

Attendance Tracking

Track daily student attendance with ease. Mark present, absent, on-leave, or out-of-station status with real-time updates.

Permission Management

Streamlined out-pass requests and approvals. Students can apply for leave, and wardens can approve or reject with comments.

Room Allocation

Manage room assignments efficiently. Track occupancy, view available beds, and manage student room transfers.

Fees Management

Track fee payments, pending dues, and generate reports. Keep financial records organized and accessible.

Food & Menu

Weekly menu management by vendors. Students can view today's menu and submit feedback on food quality.

Complaint System

Students can raise complaints which are tracked and resolved. Categorized by type for efficient handling.

Parent Visits

Track parent/visitor entries with ID verification. Maintain a log of all visits for security and records.

Circulars & Notices

Broadcast important announcements and notices to students. Stay connected with important updates.

Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports on attendance, fees, complaints, and more. Data-driven decision making made easy.
